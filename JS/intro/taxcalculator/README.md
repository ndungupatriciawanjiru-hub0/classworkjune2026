# ** Tax Calculator (2026)**

This is a simple JavaScript project I made to help calculate salary deductions in Kenya.

----------

## **What it does**

You enter your gross salary and it shows:

-   PAYE (tax)
-   NSSF
-   SHIF
-   Housing levy
-   Net salary (what you take home)

----------

## **How it works**

-   It asks you to enter your salary
-   If you enter something wrong, it asks again
-   Then it calculates all the deductions step by step

----------

## **Tax rates used**

-   0 – 24,000 → 10%
-   24,001 – 32,333 → 25%
-   32,334 – 500,000 → 30%
-   500,001 – 800,000 → 32.5%
-   Above 800,000 → 35%

It also subtracts the KES 2,400 personal relief from PAYE.

----------

## **Other deductions**

-   NSSF :Tier I (6% of the first kes 9,000)
-   NSSF :Tier II (6% of kes 9,001-108,000)
-   SHIF : 2.75% of gross
-   Housing levy : 1.5% of gross
-   Insurance relief: 15 %of premium (max kes 5,000 per month)
